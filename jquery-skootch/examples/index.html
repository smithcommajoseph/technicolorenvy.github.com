3. (Basic) Smart vs Dumb skootches

Smart skootches (true by default) will attempt to determine how much your indigen container will need to be moved by in order to make room for the invader container. In other words, you will get different animations based on your viewport's width.

A Dumb skootch will animate the same amount regardless of viewport size.

Note: You should resize these pages pre and post 'skootching' to see the difference between smart and dumb skootches.
