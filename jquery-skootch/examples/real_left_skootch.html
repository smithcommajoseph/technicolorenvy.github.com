The Skootch trigger
Lets say we have some really awesome content here. It's just gonna chill right here waiting for someone to see it.

Anatomy of a better Skootch

Using offset parents to aide in our positioning as well as the '.skootch-trigger-open' class that gets applied to the 'trigger' element to show current skootch state.

The HTML

<body>
  <dl id="skootch-me">
    <dt id="skootch-trigger">
      <a href="javascript:void(0)">Trigger text</a>
    </dt>

    <dd id="skootch-invader">
      Some content here.
    </dd>
  </dl>

  <div id="skootch-indigen">
    The "primary" content for the page
  </div>
</body>
